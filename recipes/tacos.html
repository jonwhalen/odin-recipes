Description

This delicious recipe was created by a locally renowned expert chef whose sole purpose is to bring happiness and joy to all those who are lucky enough to taste the wonder brought by this one of a kind, out of this world recipe. Words alone cannot describe just how delicious this taco recipe is.

Ingredients are simple and natural and preparation time is minimal. Fantastic for those who are low on time or energy at the end of a long day.

Ingredients

  • Taco Seasoning
  • Lean or extra lean ground beef
  • Corn tortilla wraps or shells
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese
  • Full-fat sour cream (restaurant style)
  • Pico de gallo or salsa

Steps

  1. Brown meat
  2. Add water and seasoning to meat
  3. Cook until finished
  4. Slap all the fixin's into your shell
  5. Eat until you can't anymore
